The Challenge

Plan a 2-week in-person road trip to close maximum deals for Delfa AI's patient recruitment management system.

What we had

  • Dataset A — 4,551 US sites with 2024 sponsor payments
  • Dataset B — 482 hot leads with contacts, competitor intel
  • 17 existing Delfa customers (reference accounts)
  • 13 competitor customers (warmest leads)

Key filter

  • Target FPOs — independent sites that make fast purchasing decisions and need recruitment help
  • Skip NPOs — academic medical centers with internal recruitment and 6-12 month procurement
  • Prioritize competitor customers — already understand and budget for the product category
